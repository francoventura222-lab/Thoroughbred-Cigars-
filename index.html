About Us

Thoroughbred Cigars Is dedicated to the legacy of elegance, tradition, and the spirit of horse racing. We craft premium cigars inspired by champions.

Our Mission & Vision

Vision: Building a legacy that bridges the rich history of thoroughbred tradition with modern sophistication.

Mission: “To craft cigars that embody the spirit of Kentucky heritage—rich, bold, and refined—by blending tradition, craftsmanship, and innovation to deliver an unforgettable smoking experience.”

Brand Identity

Thoroughbred Cigars embodies the spirit of Kentucky heritage — rich craftsmanship, refined tradition, and the timeless elegance of the Bluegrass. Our brand uses deep brown, bourbon amber, and gold accents with imagery of horses, barns, leather, and bourbon barrels to evoke a rich, nostalgic tone rooted in history.

Our Cigars

We offer cigars in three sizes: Robusto (5 x 50), Churchill (7 x 48), and Torpedo (6.5 x 52), each available in four unique, Kentucky-inspired flavors.

Bourbon Cherry

Profile: Sweet with a subtle cherry accent.

Base: Mild-to-medium tobacco blend that allows the flavor infusion to shine.

Wrapper Sourcing: Nicarguan Jalapa leaf for its naturally sweet, floral aromatics.

Southern Pecan

Profile: Nutty with warm undertones to feel more "southern".

Base: Medium bodied blend with natural nutty undertones.

Mint Julep

Profile: Floral & Sweet, with natural, cooling mint essence.

Base: A fresh, sweet, and aromatic foundation.

Availability: Seasonal spike (Spring/Summer), critical during Derby/Keeneland events.

Bourbon Barrel

Profile: Charred Oak (smokey), warm and dense aroma.

Base: Deep, oaky, medium-to-full bodied blend.

Sourcing: Uses real barrel/oak components from Kentucky cooperages.

Our target market is local to Kentucky, focusing on horse races (Keeneland, Derby) and country clubs.
